Our Mission

At Travel Tunes, our mission is to help international students in Dundee feel at home by providing an immersive cultural, social, and academic guide. Through curated audio experiences, insider tips, and local insights, we aim to make your journey smoother, your experiences richer, and your connections stronger. Whether you're discovering the city's hidden gems, navigating university life, or finding your favorite café, we're here to make Dundee feel like home — one tune at a time.
